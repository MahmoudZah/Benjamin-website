Hi I am Benjamin The Juggling Clown! Nice to meet me!

Benjamin got his start with the world-famous Moscow Circus, running away from home at just 14 years old with no money, sleeping in a shed near the circus tour buses. A Chinese magician touring with the circus invited him to become his assistant, and Benjamin eagerly accepted. He quickly learned magic and juggling and eventually earned a place at the prestigious Moscow Circus School, where he trained in team juggling, acrobatics, and clowning—making his dreams a reality. He entertained audiences as a juggler — sometimes while performing on horseback! (Though the horse decided not to make the trip to America.) Benjamin traveled all over the world with the Moscow Circus, performing across Europe, Africa, and Asia. One of his most demanding acts involved leaping onto the backs of galloping white stallions while juggling and tossing Indian clubs to a partner standing on the ground. After moving to the United States, Benjamin brought the circus spirit with him, trading the big top for birthdays, festivals, and celebrations of every kind. He’s since performed at more than 5,000 shows nationwide, along with appearances across Europe and beyond, delighting audiences young and old for decades. From his juggling to his clowning—and the laughter he brings everywhere he goes—Benjamin shows that the circus isn’t just under a tent. Wherever he performs becomes the circus, and for him, the best stage of all is your party.
